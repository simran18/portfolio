Background

I am a Behavior & Engangement Analyst at Accolade, while pursuing a professional Master's in Data Science program at UC Berkeley. I recently graduated from the Informatics (Data Science) program at the University of Washington. I enjoy building my technical & personal skills through interdisciplinary classes, research projects and diverse leadership roles.

I want to research the intersection of accessibility, immersive mixed reality (XR), and human-centered data science. I strive to explore how emerging XR and AI technology can empower people with disabilities and promote equity.

As a Data Scientist , my goal is to convey the people story behind the data using analytics, visualization and machine learning. As a Researcher, I use mixed-methods to explore challenges faced by different populations and social problems, such as Teen Mental Health using Social Robotics and Effectiveness of Woman Affinity Groups in Tech. As a XR enthusiast, I develop and design projects that create unique immersive experiences, while lowering barriers for technology use.
